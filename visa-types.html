Visa Types

Choose the right visa for your needs

Tourist Visa

For short-term visits, sightseeing, and leisure activities.

Requirements:

  • Valid passport (6+ months)
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Return flight ticket
  • Travel insurance

Processing Time:

5-7 business days

Student Visa

For international students enrolled in educational institutions.

Requirements:

  • Letter of acceptance
  • Proof of financial support
  • Academic transcripts
  • Medical insurance

Processing Time:

4-6 weeks

Business Visa

For business meetings, conferences, and negotiations.

Family Visa

For family reunification and dependent relatives.

Requirements:

  • Proof of relationship
  • Sponsor's residency status
  • Financial support evidence
  • Medical examination

Processing Time:

8-12 weeks

Transit Visa

For travelers passing through to another destination.

Requirements:

  • Confirmed onward ticket
  • Visa for final destination
  • Valid passport
  • Transit itinerary
