Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about visa applications, requirements, and processing times.

How do I apply for a visa?

You can apply for a visa through our online application system. Simply visit the Apply page, select the type of visa you need, fill out the application form, upload the required documents, and pay the application fee. You'll receive a confirmation email with your application reference number.

Can I edit my application after submission?

Once submitted, you cannot directly edit your application. If you need to make changes, please contact our support team immediately with your application reference number and details of the changes required. We'll guide you through the process of updating your application.

What documents do I need for a tourist visa?

For a tourist visa, you typically need:

  • Valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings or invitation letter)
  • Travel itinerary or return flight tickets
  • Proof of sufficient funds (bank statements)
  • Travel insurance (for some countries)

Do my documents need to be translated?

Yes, any documents not in English must be accompanied by certified translations. The translation should include a statement from the translator confirming the translation is accurate, the date of translation, the translator's full name and signature, and contact details.

How long does visa processing take?

Processing times vary depending on the visa type and current workload:

  • Tourist Visa: 5-10 business days
  • Business Visa: 7-14 business days
  • Student Visa: 4-6 weeks
  • Work Visa: 6-8 weeks

These are estimated times and may vary. You can check your application status anytime on our Track Application page.

Can I expedite my visa application?

Expedited processing may be available for certain visa types in emergency situations (medical emergencies, family crises, or urgent business needs). Additional fees apply for expedited service. To request expedited processing, please contact our support team with your application reference number and documentation supporting your request.

What are the visa application fees?

Our current visa application fees are:

  • Tourist Visa: $120
  • Business Visa: $150
  • Student Visa: $200
  • Work Visa: $250
  • Transit Visa: $60

Fees are non-refundable, even if the application is refused. Payment methods include credit/debit cards and bank transfers.

Is the visa fee refundable if my application is denied?

No, the visa application fee is non-refundable regardless of the outcome of your application. This fee covers the administrative costs of processing your application, which are incurred whether the visa is granted or not.
